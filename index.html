About

Hi! My name is Jasmina and I am an aspiring Front End Developer.

As a recent graduate of Thinkful.com, I have studied HTML, CSS, jQuery, JavaScript, and Ajax. Take a look at some of the projects I have worked on in the Portfolio section.

Originally Croatian/Serbian, I have lived in the Middle East for the past 22 years, with a five year break during which time I was getting my Bachelor's degree in the US. I then returned to the Middle East where I worked and traveled around the region.

Living and working in the Middle East has given me an opportunity to meet people from different backgrounds and cultures.

Resume

After graduating from Northern Michigan University with a Bachelor's degree in Computer Information Systems, I moved to Oman and started my career in the telecom industry, where I spent 9 years working as a service engineer.

As a service engineer, I have worked with greenfield operators as well as global operators to help them develop and expand their solutions that would best serve their customers.

After 9 years of working as an engineer, I wanted to expand my knowledge and made a move to project management.
